So why photography?

Who cares about photography! Why spend effort learning techniques and imaging software? Why spend so much money on camera equipment when there are smartphones?

Inspiration

My motivation lies with time. Capturing any moment, whether boring or interesting, is something worth looking back on.

Aspiration

The goal of photography is to create perfection. However, I prefer to keep my photos simple. No need to make things dramatic and too artisitic, the best is usually what you have.

Perserverance

Practice makes perfect. Most photos come out unappealing; only a select few ever make the cut. Nobody should be discouraged just because they cannot achieve a wonderful photo every time they shoot.
